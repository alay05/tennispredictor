# Requirements Archive: v1.0 MVP

**Archived:** 2026-06-25
**Outcome:** All v1 requirements were delivered by the v1.0 milestone and are retained here for historical reference.

## Delivered Requirement Groups

### Foundation

- FND-01 through FND-06: project scaffolding, Sackmann source metadata, schema validation, canonical tables, and ATP-only quarantine rules.

### Feature Engine

- FEAT-01 through FEAT-09: chronological feature emission, Elo, rankings, recent form, serve/return, head-to-head, match context, differential features, and leakage tests.

### Modeling

- MOD-01 through MOD-08: frozen chronological datasets, baseline models, XGBoost candidate, calibration, evaluation metrics, segment diagnostics, and artifact persistence.

### Backtesting

- BKT-01 through BKT-06: replay harness, reusable EV decision logic, reason-coded opportunities, backtest metrics, provenance labels, and guarded profitability reporting.

### Kalshi

- KAL-01 through KAL-05: authenticated read client, snapshot persistence, retry and rate-limit behavior, DTO encapsulation, and read-only collection guardrails.

### Market Mapping and EV

- MKT-01 through MKT-08: player normalization, alias overrides, mapping states, ambiguity rejection, market probability source capture, executable EV calculations, live/shadow reuse, and opportunity ranking.

### Alerts and Operations

- OPS-01 through OPS-06: terminal and persisted opportunity reports, configurable one-shot settings, correlated audit logging, CLI commands, quality gates, and operator documentation.
